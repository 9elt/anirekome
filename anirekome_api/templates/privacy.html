PRIVACY POLICY (effective date 4/4/2022)

1 - USER'S COLLECTED INFORMATION

anirekome.com collects the following information:

  • user's myanimelist.net username
  • user's myanimelist.net animelist
anirekome.com does NOT use trackers, therefore user's information such as location, browsing history etc. will NOT be collect

In compliance with the myanimelist.net "API License and Developer Agreement" anirekome.com does NOT collect nor store any sensitive information about its users (user credentials, emails, phone numbers, etc).

2 - USER'S STORED INFORMATION

anirekome.com permanently stores the following information:

  • user's myanimelist.net username (to prevent animelist duplicates)
  • user's myanimelist.net animelist
  • user's recommendation model created by anirekome.com
anirekome.com temporarily stores (in database sessions) the following information:
  • user's preferences (include, or not, plan to watch to recommendations)
  • user's recommendations
  • user's deleted recommendations
anirekome.com ONLY stores PUBLICLY available information, if your myanimelist.net animelist is set to PRIVATE anirekome.com will NOT store any of the information listed above.

3 - USE OF THE USER'S INFORMATION

In compliance with the myanimelist.net "API License and Developer Agreement", anirekome.com will NOT sell or make available to third parties any of the information collected or stored.

Note that by using anirekome.com you AGREE the previously listed information (points 1 and 2) to be collected and/or stored for the ONLY purpose of making anirekome.com able to deliver and improve its services.

4 - ADVERTISMENT

anirekome.com does NOT sell any advertising space nor is willing to do so.
